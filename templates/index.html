Welcome to the WORD GAME

On this website you can check the current rankings.
After a successful login in the terminal you will be given a chance to join the game.

WORD GAME RULES:

1. You have to send your reply within 10 seconds not to be kicked out of the server

2. You have to send your reply within 2 seconds not to be ignored by the server

3. Queuing time lasts 5 minutes maximally. After this stage a game will try to get launched.

4. The game will start automatically when a queue timer stops and the queue contains of at least 2 ACTIVE players or when 10 players join the queue.

5. One player at a time will be asked to choose a word for others to guess until the word is being chosen or there are no more players left in the game.

6. The word has to be validated with a dictionary to be accepted by the server

7. Each player can choose one of 2 options during the game:
[+] to guess a letter, or
[=] to guess the word.

8. Each guessed letter will give the player n * 1 point, where n means number of occurances of the chosen letter in the word

9. Guessing the word will give the player a total of 5 points

10. Each try of not following the instructions or maliciously inserted data will result in the player being kicked out of the server
